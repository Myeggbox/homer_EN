Feature One

Easily add documents about your belongings, your home or insurance using an app-integrated scanner, and let Homer dealing with the search of the important information.

Moreover, Myeggbox allows you to connect your e-commerce and insurance accounts in order to gather all your invoices and contracts. Homer then synchronizes in real time your account and updates the information.

Feature Two

Each of your product has its own dedicated space in which you can find a whole range of services :

  • Warranty Alert
  • User manuals
  • After-sales service phone number
  • Videos and Tutorials
  • Lend, sell and replace your item
  • Accessories and consumables

Feature Three

Your belongings are sorted by residence and you will find information related to the latter (purchase price, floor area, number of rooms); important documents (notarial deed, work quotation, leases, etc.) and useful services for :

  • Moving in and out
  • Caretaking
  • DIY
  • Works
  • And more

Feature Four

Homer clarifies your insurance by analyzing your contracts of property insurance. Like this, you will know the exclusions in your contract, the price of your franchises and preventive measurement recommended by your insurer.

In case of disaster, Homer supports you in the declaration and allows you to transfer all the documents that your insurer asks for in one click.
