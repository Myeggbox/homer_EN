Frequently Asked Questions

How does the report given by Homer works ?

Homer analyzes the information given by your insurer as well as from the belongings that you saved in the Myeggbox app, and crosses the information in order to measure for you if your insurance contract is optimized according to your needs. Your warranties, covers and exclusions are displayed as simply as possible in a dashboard, explaining everything you need to know.

Does buying with Homer cause an increase of cost ?

The bonus to get when you buy with Homer does not increase the price you have to pay. You only have to pay the price of the product, while obtaining bonus.

Is my insurer compatible with Homer ?

Homer works with every French insurers. The automatic synchronization is available for a majority of insurers. It also is possible to manually add the general and special terms and conditions in order to allow Homer to perform his analyse. For the moment, the synchronization is not available yet for bank insurances.

What are the differences between the free and paid offers ?

You can freely access your invoices, user manuals, and consumables of your most important belongings. For 3.29€ per month, Home (the personnal assistant of the app) will support you in the optimisation of the protection of these belongings, as well as informing you on the different kinds of covers available.

Is Homer available on my computer ?

At the moment, Homer is exclusively available on the Myeggbox mobile app. Your Myeggbox desktop space allows you to access you imortant documents.
